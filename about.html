Website Story:

Alterego website is a school project tool. A materialization of product based on the knowledge gain from the past lectures. This is a final output for the first semester in the subject, Understanding The Self.


Goal:

The goal of this tool is to give a self-awareness to a person. To reflect from their self, it could be their thoughts, actions, emotions, body, and feelings. It will help them to become familliar to the self, and will make them more responsible.
